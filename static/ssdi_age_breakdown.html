SSDI by Age: Who Could Actually Return to Work?

7.2M disabled workers segmented by age and disability type — December 2024

Of the 7.2 million disabled workers, 80% are over 50 — most will age into Social Security retirement, not return to work.
The addressable RTW pool is the 1.47 million under 50 — and 49% of them have mental health conditions, the most compatible with remote and AI-adjacent work.
