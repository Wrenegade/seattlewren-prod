Assumptions: Avg SSDI monthly benefit: $1,540 ($18,480/yr). Avg Medicare/Medicaid cost per SSDI beneficiary: ~$3,000/yr partial savings. New worker productivity: 65% of national avg GDP per worker ($172,600 → ~$112,200). Estimated tax contribution: 12% effective rate on median re-entry wage (~$38K → ~$4,600/yr). Tax credit is a one-time Year 1 cost; savings recur annually. Year 2+ assumes 80% retention (20% return to SSDI). Real outcomes depend on which beneficiaries return (back injuries vs. severe disability), local labor markets, employer participation, and individual circumstances.
