Assumptions: Current figures use 2024 estimates — GDP $29.0T, Federal Debt $35.5T, Civilian Labor Force 168.0M, Civilian Noninstitutional Population 268.0M. GDP per worker calculated as GDP ÷ labor force = ~$172,600. Debt held constant (no new borrowing or paydown) to isolate the GDP growth effect. Real-world results would vary based on what industries absorb new workers, wage levels, time to full productivity, and fiscal policy.

Baseline data: BLS, BEA, OMB
