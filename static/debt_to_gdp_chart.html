U.S. Gross Federal Debt as % of GDP

1939–2023  |  The debt didn't get "paid off" after WWII. The economy just outgrew it.

Source: FRED / U.S. Office of Management and Budget (GFDGDPA188S)

119% → 31% Post-WWII peak to 1974 low. GDP boomed, the ratio shrank for 30 years — without ever "paying it off."
31% → 119% 1974 to 2023. Defense buildup, 2008 financial crisis, COVID pandemic. Right back where we started.
