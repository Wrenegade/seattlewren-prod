Hello! I'm Jon Wren, a Seattle-based technologist with a passion for AI, data engineering, and the art of building things. This blog is my digital garden—a space where I cultivate ideas, share projects, and document my journey through technology and creativity.

Professionally, I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence and data systems, solving complex problems and building scalable solutions. Personally, I explore hobbies that range from camping & fishing, bicycle touring, cooking, gardening, music production and exploring all things in the beautiful Pacific Northwest.

What you'll find here is a mix of technical deep-dives, project showcases, hobby explorations, and general musings on life and learning. I believe in learning by doing, teaching by sharing, and growing by connecting with others.

## What to Expect

- **Musings:** Mixed bag on lifestyle, productivity, hobbies, and healthy recipes
- **AI:** Articles, personal projects, and insights from my practice and research in AI
- **Data:** Reports, dashboards, and insights I find interesting
- **Social:** Thoughts on politics, society, and ways to make an impact

I'm always excited to connect with fellow creators, thinkers, and learners. Whether you're here for technical content, creative inspiration, or just browsing, I hope you find something that resonates with you.

**Thanks for stopping by, and happy reading!**
