U.S. Federal Debt Held by the Public as % of GDP

1939–2023  |  The debt didn't get "paid off" after WWII. The economy just outgrew it.

Source: FRED / U.S. Office of Management and Budget (FYPUGDA188S)

106% → 22% Post-WWII peak to 1974 low. GDP boomed, the ratio shrank for 35 years — without ever "paying it off."
22% → 94% 1974 to 2023. Three surges: Reagan defense buildup, 2008 financial crisis, COVID pandemic.
